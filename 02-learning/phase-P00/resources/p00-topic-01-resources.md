## 🗺️ The Map (Before You Read Anything)

```
AI (big umbrella)
└── ML (learns from data)
    └── DL (neural networks, complex patterns)
        └── GenAI (creates NEW content: text, image, code)
            └── Agentic AI (acts autonomously, uses tools) ← WHERE WE'RE GOING
```

**Non-tech analogy:**
- AI = Smart employee
- ML = Employee who learns from experience
- DL = Employee with a massive brain who finds hidden patterns
- GenAI = Employee who creates original work
- Agentic AI = Employee who works independently, no supervision
